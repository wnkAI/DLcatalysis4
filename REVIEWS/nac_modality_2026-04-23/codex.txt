  Why it matters: these coverage metrics will read as almost-always `1.0` and can hide missing-modality/dropout/data-quality problems the diagnostics were added to catch.  
  Suggested fix: log per-sample fractions instead, e.g. `atom_mask.any(dim=1).float().mean()`, `pocket_mask.any(dim=1).float().mean()`, and a true per-sample RXN availability mask rather than `hasattr(...)`.
