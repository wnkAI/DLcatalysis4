  The new inverted-scaled `keep_*` mask is reused at embed level, token level, and output level, so kept samples are amplified multiple times (`rxn_emb`, `pocket_pool`, `annot_emb`, `pocket_tokens`, then `y_*` again) instead of satisfying the claimed `E[keep * x] == x`.
